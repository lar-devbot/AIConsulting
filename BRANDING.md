# BRANDING.md — Urban, Cool, Professional

## Visual Identity

**Vibe**: NYC tech founder who ships. Confident, minimalist, no hype. Works fast, thinks big.

**Core Aesthetic**:
- **Clean lines**, minimal decoration
- **Dark mode friendly** (dark grays, blacks, bright accents)
- **Geometric simplicity** (circles, grids, clean typography)
- **Urban edge** without looking edgy (professional >always)
- **Tech-first** (feels like a serious AI company, not a consultant)

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Charcoal Black** `#1a1a1a` — Primary (text, headers)
- **Deep Navy Blue** `#0a1f3f` — Accent (buttons, highlights)
- **Electric Blue** `#00d9ff` — Highlight/Energy (calls-to-action, hover states)

### Secondary Colors
- **Clean White** `#ffffff` — Backgrounds
- **Light Gray** `#f5f5f5` — Subtle backgrounds
- **Muted Silver** `#a0a0a0` — Muted text

### Accent Colors
- **Tech Cyan** `#00d9ff` (electricity, AI energy)
- **Minimal Orange** `#ff6b35` (warmth, human touch) — use sparingly

### Do NOT Use
- Red (aggressive)
- Bright greens (startup cliché)
- Pastels (too soft for "professional")

---

## Typography

### Headlines
- **Font**: "Inter" or "Helvetica Neue" (clean, modern, NYC feels)
- **Weight**: Bold 600-700
- **Size**: Large and confident (minimum 18px for LinkedIn headlines)
- **Example**: "AI for Impatient Founders — Production Grade in Weeks"

### Body Text
- **Font**: "Inter" or "System Font" (clarity first)
- **Weight**: Regular 400-500
- **Size**: 14-16px (readable)
- **Tracking**: Slightly loose (professional, not cramped)

### Buttons/CTAs
- **Font**: Bold sans-serif
- **Text**: "AUDIT NOW" or "SCHEDULE CALL" (short, action-oriented)

---

## Visual Style Guide

### Photography/Imagery
- **Style**: Minimalist, geometric, tech-forward
- **Subjects**: Abstract code, clean workspaces, process diagrams (NOT cheesy stock photos)
- **Filters**: Clean, high contrast, slightly desaturated (not over-processed)
- **Avoid**: Smiling people in suits, "teamwork" stock photos, bright office spaces

### Icons & Graphics
- **Style**: Simple line icons, geometric shapes
- **Colors**: Charcoal + Electric Blue + White
- **Weight**: Consistent line weight (1.5-2px)
- **Spacing**: Generous, clean breathing room

### Layout Principles
- **Grid-based**: Clean alignment (8px/16px grid)
- **Whitespace**: Lots of it. Luxury = space
- **Asymmetric balance**: Not perfectly symmetrical, but intentional
- **Movement**: Subtle animations (fade in, slide, no bounce)

### Profile Picture (400 × 400 px)

**Current approach**: Professional headshot (you)

**Styling**:
- **Background**: Charcoal black or solid navy blue (no office background)
- **Framing**: Shoulders + head (LinkedIn standard)
- **Lighting**: Clean, professional (well-lit, no shadows)
- **Dress code**: Dark sweater or blazer (navy, charcoal, black) + maybe subtle tech vibe (glasses, clean look)
- **Expression**: Confident, direct gaze (not smiling, not stern — thoughtful founder energy)
- **Post-processing**: High contrast, slightly desaturated, professional tone

**If new photo needed**:
- Get a professional headshot (fast, ~$50-200)
- OR use existing best photo + crop/edit for clean background

**DO NOT USE**:
- Selfies
- Casual photos
- Overly filtered/bright photos
- Group photos
