# PLAN.md — 90-Day Roadmap: $0 → $30k/Month

## Overview

This 90-day plan takes you from positioning to revenue. The strategy is:

1. **Build credibility** (positioning, content, social proof)
2. **Validate the market** (free audits, collect feedback, iterate messaging)
3. **Productize offerings** (clear service packages, pricing, case studies)
4. **Launch funnel** (landing page, quiz, lead generation)
5. **Scale to $30k/month** (close deals, optimize, refine)

## Phase 2: Market Validation & Free Audits (Week 3-4)

### Goal
Run 10 free "AI Opportunity Audits" to validate positioning, refine messaging, and build case studies.

### Key Actions

#### Week 3

**[ ] Free Audit Offer Go-Live** (1 hour)
- [ ] Update LinkedIn headline to include: "Offering free AI Opportunity Assessments this month"
- [ ] Create simple landing page (Carrd or one-pager) or Calendly link for scheduling audits
- [ ] Post offer across channels: LinkedIn, email to your network, DM to warm leads

**[ ] Run 5 Free Audits** (20 hours)
- [ ] Each audit: 1-hour discovery call + 1-hour analysis + 30-min follow-up email with findings
- [ ] Template discovery questions (see Audit Template below)
- [ ] Capture 2 key outcomes per audit:
  - **Biggest AI opportunity** (what process to automate first)
  - **ROI estimate** (potential time/cost savings)

**[ ] Audit Template (Use This)**

```
DISCOVERY CALL (60 min)
1. Business overview: Size, revenue, team structure
2. Current pain points: Top 3 manual/repetitive tasks
3. Constraints: Budget, timeline, technical skill, risk tolerance
4. Desired outcomes: What would success look like?
5. Technology stack: What systems are they using? (CRM, email, etc.)

ANALYSIS (Offline, 60 min)
1. Identify top 3 AI opportunities (ranked by ROI)
2. For each: Process flow, data requirements, risk factors
3. Estimate impact: Time saved, cost savings, revenue impact
4. High-level architecture (how you'd build it)
5. Timeline + cost estimate for implementation

FOLLOW-UP (30 min async)
1. One-page summary of findings
2. Recommended next steps (if any)
3. Offer: "If you decide to move forward, here's what engagement looks like..."
4. Ask: "Who else should I talk to? Referrals?"
5. Case study offer: "Can I share your results (anonymously) as a case study?"
```

#### Week 4

**[ ] Run 5 More Free Audits** (20 hours)
- [ ] Repeat Week 3 process
- [ ] Refine discovery questions based on learnings
- [ ] Build 2-3 light case study summaries (with permission)

**[ ] Collect & Document Feedback** (4 hours)
- [ ] After each audit, note:
  - Most common pain points (you'll see patterns)
  - Budget they mentioned
  - Hesitations / objections (capture these)
  - Whether they'd move to paid engagement (Y/N, why?)
  
**[ ] Refine Messaging** (3 hours)
- [ ] Based on 10 audits, update MESSAGING.md with:
  - Real language prospects use (use their words in your positioning)
  - Top 3-5 AI use cases (what's actually in demand)
  - Objections you heard + how to overcome them

#### Phase 2 Metrics

- **Free audits completed**: 10
- **Case studies initiated**: 2-3
- **Prospect feedback**: 10 conversations = rich qualitative data
- **Referrals generated**: 5+ (goal)
- **Willing to pay**: 2-3 out of 10 (this is success — validates demand)

## Phase 3: Productization & Pricing (Week 5-6)

### Goal
Define service packages, pricing, and create collateral to make selling easier.

### Key Actions

#### Week 5

**[ ] Define Service Packages** (6 hours)
Create 3 tiers:

**Tier 1: AI Opportunity Audit** ($0 → $3k)
- 2-hour discovery + analysis + 1-page findings
- Identifies top automation opportunities
- Includes recommendation for next steps
- Goal: Filter/qualify prospects

**Tier 2: AI Agent Implementation** ($10-15k project + $5k/month retainer)
- 6-week engagement to design, build, and deploy 1 AI agent
- Includes: Architecture, development, testing, training, handoff
- Example: Build customer support agent that reduces support tickets by 60%
- Post-launch: $5k/month retainer for monitoring, optimization, feature requests

**Tier 3: AI Transformation Package** ($30-40k project + $8k/month retainer)
- 12-week engagement to automate 2-3 processes across the org
- Multiple AI agents + integration with existing systems
- Includes: Change management, team training, documentation
- Post-launch: $8k/month for ongoing optimization and expansion

**[ ] Create Service One-Pagers** (4 hours)
- For each tier: 1 PDF or Notion page with:
  - What's included (deliverables, timeline, process)
  - Who it's for (ICP: company size, industry, pain point)
  - Cost (project + monthly)
  - Expected ROI/impact
  - Next steps to engage

**[ ] Pricing Rationale** (2 hours)
- Document in FINANCIALS.md:
  - How you arrived at these prices
  - Margins, delivery costs, labor allocation
  - Why this undercuts enterprise but commands premium vs. commodity consultants

#### Week 6

**[ ] Build Case Studies** (8 hours)
- From the 10 audits, pick 2-3 where you got permission to document results
- Create 1-page case study for each:
  - Client (anonymized or with permission)
  - Challenge / pain point
  - Solution (what AI agent you'd build)
  - Expected impact / ROI
  - Testimonial (real quote if possible)
  - CTA: "Want similar results?"

**[ ] Create Sales Deck / One-Sheet** (4 hours)
- 1-page visual summary of:
  - Problem: "You're losing [X] hours/week to manual [process]"
  - Solution: "AI agent that handles it automatically"
  - Proof: 1-2 case studies showing impact
  - Cost: "Investment: $[X]. Savings: $[Y]. Payback: [Z] months"
  - CTA: "Book a free AI Opportunity Assessment"

**[ ] FAQs / Objection Handling** (3 hours)
- Document common questions from your 10 audits:
  - "How is this different from no-code automation tools?"
  - "What if the AI makes mistakes?"
  - "How long does implementation really take?"
  - "What if we want to change vendors later?"
- Write clear, confident answers

#### Phase 3 Metrics

- **Service packages defined**: 3
- **Case studies drafted**: 2-3
- **Sales collateral created**: Sales deck + FAQs
- **Pricing rationalized**: Unit economics documented

## Phase 4: Funnel & Lead Generation (Week 7-8)

### Goal
Launch landing page + quiz funnel to systematize lead capture and qualification.

### Key Actions

#### Week 7

**[ ] Landing Page** (8 hours)
Build on Webflow, Carrd, or WordPress:

**Page structure**:
1. **Hero** (above fold)
   - Headline: "Fire Your Employees. No, Wait... Fire Me Instead."
   - Subheading: "AI agents do the repetitive work. Your team does the thinking."
   - CTA: "Take the AI Readiness Quiz" → leads to quiz

2. **Problem section**
   - "You're losing $X per month to manual [process]"
   - Paint the pain (your audit data shows the real problems)

3. **Solution section**
   - How agentic AI fixes it
   - Show the payoff: "80% of routine work automated"

4. **How it works** (3-step process)
   - Step 1: Free AI Opportunity Audit
   - Step 2: We build the AI agent (6 weeks)
   - Step 3: Hands-off operation + optimization

5. **Social proof**
   - 2-3 case studies (name/logo if possible, anonymized if not)
   - "X% reduction in manual work"
   - Testimonial quotes

6. **Pricing** (transparent)
   - 3 tiers visible
   - "Not sure? Start with a free audit"

7. **FAQ**
   - Address top objections

8. **Footer CTA**
   - "Ready to automate? Book a free audit"
   - Or: "Take the AI Readiness Quiz"

**[ ] Quiz Funnel** (6 hours)
Use Typeform, Interact, or custom form:

**Quiz flow**:
- **Q1**: "How many hours/week do you spend on [repetitive task]?"
  - Options: <5h, 5-10h, 10-20h, 20+h
- **Q2**: "Is this [task] a priority to automate?"
  - Options: Not yet, Maybe, Yes, Urgent
- **Q3**: "What's your timeline?"
  - Options: <3 months, 3-6 months, 6-12 months, Just exploring
- **Q4**: "What's your budget?"
  - Options: <$5k, $5-15k, $15-50k, 50k+
- **Q5**: "What's your industry?"
  - Open text (helps with targeting)

**Quiz results**:
- Auto-personalized summary showing if they're a good fit
- Score 1-5 (1=not ready, 5=perfect fit)
- Segmented CTA:
  - If score 4-5: "You're ready. Book your AI Strategy Session ($3k value, free)"
  - If score 2-3: "Let's chat. Free 20-min consultation to explore fit"
  - If score 1: "Thanks for taking the quiz. Here's a free resource to get started..."

#### Week 8

**[ ] Set Up Lead Routing** (3 hours)
- Quiz results auto-email to you
- High-fit leads (score 4-5) trigger email sequence:
  - Email 1 (immediate): Quiz results + calendar link to book
  - Email 2 (3 days later): "Still interested? Here's what to expect..."
  - Email 3 (7 days later): "One more shot — let's see if we're a fit"

**[ ] Traffic Campaign Launch** (5 hours)
- [ ] LinkedIn ads targeting SMB founders/CEOs
  - Budget: $300-500/week
  - Goal: Get 20+ quiz starts from ads
  - Offer: "Take the AI Readiness Quiz (30 seconds)"
- [ ] Post about landing page + quiz on LinkedIn (organic)
- [ ] Email to network: "I launched this thing, take the quiz and send me feedback"

**[ ] Email Nurture Sequence** (4 hours)
For leads who don't immediately book:
- Nurture 5-email sequence (1 email every 3-4 days)
- Content: Success stories, AI education, addressing objections
- Goal: Re-engage cold leads, move them to warm
- Track clicks and opens; note high-engagement prospects

#### Phase 4 Metrics

- **Landing page**: Live and indexed
- **Quiz**: Live, collecting responses
- **Leads captured**: 30+
- **High-fit leads (score 4-5)**: 5-10
- **Meetings booked**: 2-3 (goal)

### Key Actions

#### Week 9

**[ ] Sales Discovery Calls** (20 hours)

  - Explore scope (which process to automate first?)

  - Decision-maker (who approves?)

- Kickoff call: Explain process, set expectations, collect info
- Start architecture/design phase
- Document process (this becomes your repeatable playbook)

#### Week 10

**[ ] Continue Sales Calls** (15 hours)
- Convert 1-2 more warm leads
- Close 1-2 more deals
- Adjust messaging based on what's working

**[ ] Deliver First Client Results** (15 hours)
- Build initial AI agent for first client
- Test, iterate, refine
- Prepare for launch + handoff

#### Week 11-12

**[ ] Scale Pipeline** (20 hours)
- Continue sales activities (calls, follow-ups, new leads)
- Get first 3-5 clients to launch phase (agents in production)
- Build 2-3 more agents for different clients
- Collect testimonials, results, case studies

**[ ] Optimize & Iterate** (10 hours)
- What's working in your sales message? Double down.
- What's not? Kill it.

- Update pricing if needed

**[ ] Document Playbook** (8 hours)
- How to discovery call, qualify, scope, build, launch
- Templates for contracts, SOWs, onboarding
- This becomes your repeatable system (key to scaling)

## Key Success Factors

1. **Consistency**: 3x per week content, daily network outreach (first 4 weeks)
2. **Real conversations**: Do the 10 free audits; don't skip this
3. **Speed**: Move from positioning → validation → sales ASAP
4. **Feedback loops**: What leads say → adjust messaging → test again
5. **Social proof**: Get testimonials early; use them to close later deals
6. **Referrals**: Each client should send you 2-3 warm introductions
7. **Playbook**: Document everything; don't reinvent each engagement

---

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| "No one books audits" | Pivot to outbound (personal DMs to 50 prospects) + lower ask (free 20-min call) |
| "Audits don't convert" | Revisit messaging; they may not believe you yet. Add case studies, testimonials |
| "Can't actually build what you promised" | Be honest; start with smaller scope. Use white-label partners if needed |
| "Sales calls don't close" | Improve discovery; maybe you're talking to wrong people. Tighten ICP |
| "Delivery takes too long" | Productize the hell out of it. Build repeatable templates, pre-trained agents |
