## Tier 1: AI Opportunity Audit

### What It Is
A structured discovery and analysis process to identify your highest-ROI AI automation opportunities, validate market fit, and give you a clear roadmap.

### Who It's For
- Companies curious about AI but unsure where to start
- Businesses that have tried AI and got stuck
- Decision-makers who need justification for the investment
- Companies with limited budget (under $20k)

### What You Get

**Discovery Call (1 hour)**
- Overview of your business, team, revenue
- Top 3 pain points (manual/repetitive work)
- Current tech stack and tools
- Budget, timeline, and decision-making process

**Analysis & Report (1 hour offline)**
- Identify top 3-5 AI automation opportunities (ranked by ROI)
- For each: Process flow, data requirements, risk factors, expected impact
- High-level architecture (not detailed, just "here's how we'd build it")
- Estimated timeline and cost for implementation
- One-pager: timeline + cost + expected ROI

**Presentation (30 min async)**
- One-page report via email
- Top recommendation with ROI estimate
- "Next steps if you want to proceed"
- Offer: "Want to build this? Here's how we'd work together"

### Investment

**$0-3,000** (variable based on complexity)
- Free: If referred by an existing client
- $1,500: Standard audit
- $2,500-3,000: Complex business with multiple potential projects

### Timeline
2 weeks from intake to report delivery

### Expected Outcome
- Clear understanding of AI opportunities (top 3)
- Confidence that AI can help (or clarity that timing isn't right)
- Specific ROI estimate for your best opportunity
- A roadmap to move forward (if desired)

### Success Case
> Company: Mid-sized SaaS (12 people, $2M ARR)
> 
> **Before**: Knew AI could help but didn't know where
> 
> **Audit findings**:
> 1. Customer support: Save 15 hours/week ($60k/year) → Easy to build, 6-week timeline
> 2. Sales lead qualification: Save 8 hours/week ($32k/year) → Medium complexity
> 3. Content creation: Save 10 hours/week ($40k/year) → Complex, longer timeline
> 
> **Action**: Client decided to proceed with #1 (support agent)
> 
> **Engagement**: Tier 2 implementation (see below)

## Tier 2: AI Agent Implementation

### What It Is
Design, build, test, and deploy a production-grade AI agent that automates a specific business process. Includes integration with your existing tools, team training, and 3-month ongoing optimization.

### Who It's For
- Companies with a clear, high-impact pain point to solve
- Businesses with $15-25k budget for one project
- Teams ready to implement (not just exploring)
- SMBs (10-100 people) with established processes

### What You Get

**Phase 1: Discovery & Design (Weeks 1-2)**
- Detailed process mapping (how the process currently works)
- Data requirements (what data does the AI need?)
- Success metrics (how will we measure success?)
- Integration mapping (what systems will the agent connect to?)
- Technical architecture design
- Project plan and timeline

**Phase 2: Build (Weeks 3-4)**
- Custom AI agent development
- Training on your specific data (if applicable)
- Integration with your tools (CRM, email, helpdesk, accounting, etc.)
- Error handling and edge cases
- Performance testing

**Phase 3: Launch (Week 5-6)**
- Staging environment testing
- Team training and documentation
- Soft launch (limited scope, close monitoring)
- Full production launch
- Initial monitoring and optimization

**Ongoing (Months 4-12)**
- Weekly check-ins (first month)
- Performance monitoring and alerts
- Bug fixes and refinements
- Feature requests and expansion
- Cost optimization

### Investment

**Project Phase: $15,000** (covers 6 weeks of design, build, test, launch)
**Retainer: $5,000/month** (Months 1-3)
**Retainer: $3,000-5,000/month** (Months 4-12, based on usage)

**Total Year 1: $75,000**

### Timeline
6 weeks from kickoff to live + 3-12 months ongoing

### What's Included

- [ ] Custom AI agent (trained to your specs)
- [ ] Integration with 1-2 of your existing systems (API connections)
- [ ] Architecture and technical documentation
- [ ] Team training and runbooks
- [ ] First 90 days of support and optimization
- [ ] Performance reporting (weekly dashboards)
- [ ] Up to 5 feature requests or refinements (post-launch)

### What's NOT Included

- Ongoing feature development beyond 5 requests/quarter
- Major scope changes (if it turns into a different system)
- Integration with more than 2 systems (additional cost)
- Custom training data preparation (you provide or we charge hourly)

### Success Metrics

Success is measured in concrete ROI:

**Example: Support Agent**
- Metric: Hours/week of support staff time
- Before: 40 hours/week (1 FTE)
- After: 8 hours/week (escalations, complex issues)
- Savings: 32 hours/week = $30k-50k/year

**Example: Lead Qualification**
- Metric: % of leads qualified by AI
- Before: 0% (all manual)
- After: 85% qualified automatically
- Savings: 15 hours/week sales time = $30k/year

**Example: Data Entry**
- Metric: Accuracy and speed of data extraction
- Before: 2 hours/week, 95% accuracy (human)
- After: 15 min/week, 90% extraction (AI), 5 min human review
- Savings: 1.75 hours/week = $9k/year

>

> 
> **Investment**: $15k + $5k/mo
> **Payback**: 7 months
> **Ongoing value**: $20k+/year recurring

- Entry point to build trust
- Leads to Tier 2 (larger deals)
- May be free for referrals (word-of-mouth leverage)

**Tier 2 ($15k + $5k/mo)**
- $15k project phase covers your time (design, build, test, launch = ~200 hours at $75/hr)

- Typical engagement: $75k Year 1 revenue

- $40k project phase covers larger scope (design, build 3 agents, launch, training = ~500 hours)
- $8k/month retainer reflects larger organizational impact and ongoing support
- Typical engagement: $130k+ Year 1 revenue

## Sales Approach

### Tier 1 → Tier 2 Conversion Flow

1. **Offer Tier 1** (low commitment, high curiosity)
   - "Free or $1.5k AI Opportunity Audit"
   - 95% conversion to exploration
2. **Deliver great Audit** (build credibility)
   - Clear findings, specific ROI, honest recommendations
   - 30-40% of audits recommend moving to Tier 2
3. **If Tier 2 qualifies** (they have budget + urgency)
   - "Here's exactly how we'd build this..."
   - Proposal with timeline, deliverables, cost
   - 70% of qualified leads close on Tier 2

### Tier 2 → Tier 3 Upsell Flow

1. **During Tier 2 engagement** (show value)
   - Agent launches, delivers results
   - Client sees the power of automation
2. **After 3 months** (they see ROI)
   - "You've seen what one agent can do. What if we did this for 3 processes?"
   - Tier 3 becomes obvious next step
3. **Upsell** (higher confidence, higher value)
   - 40-50% of Tier 2 clients upgrade to Tier 3 within 6 months

## Rejection & Qualification

### When to Recommend Tier 1
- Budget unclear
- Decision-maker not in the room
- Vague problem statement ("We need to use AI somehow")
- Not urgent (nice-to-have, not pain point)

### When to Recommend Tier 2
- Clear pain point (quantifiable, concrete)
- Budget $15k+ available
- Timeline matters (6-week timeline fits their need)
- Decision-maker present and interested
- Process is well-defined

### When to Recommend Tier 3
- Multiple pain points (3+)
- Budget $40k+ available
- Organizational change is okay (team ready to adapt)
- They want to be AI-forward (not just solve one problem)
- Long-term relationship desired

### When to Walk Away
- Budget < $5k (too small to deliver real value)
- No clear ROI (can't measure success)
- Organization not ready (no buy-in, resistance to change)
- Process is too vague/undefined
- Non-technical founder who won't empower technical team
