## 🚨 Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| "No one takes the free audits" | Pivot to cold outreach (personal DMs to 50 prospects) |
| "Free audits don't convert to paid" | Revisit positioning; they may not believe yet. Add testimonials. |
| "Can't deliver what you promised" | Be honest; start smaller. Use white-label partners if needed. |
| "Sales calls don't close" | Improve qualification; maybe wrong audience. Tighten ICP. |
| "Delivery takes too long" | Build templates, pre-trained agents, reusable modules. |

## 🎓 Key Lessons from This Research

1. **Market Timing is Right**: AI consulting market growing 26%+/year; SMBs are completely underserved.

2. **Your Background is an Advantage**: Most AI consultants can't actually build. Your software engineering background is a credible differentiator.

3. **Focus > Breadth**: Agentic AI + SMB automation is a narrower lane than "general AI consulting," but it's also more defensible and more immediately valuable to clients.

4. **Outcome-Based Pricing**: This single decision (charging for results, not hours) will separate you from 90% of consultants. It builds trust and justifies premium pricing.

5. **Playbook Before Growth**: Document your process early. This enables you to scale without burning out.
